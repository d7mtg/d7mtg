BHY Property Management

  SERVICES



BHY Property Management is a high-end property management company based in New Jersey.

They've been in the business for years, and hold a strong reputation for providing quality services.

BHY Property Management approached us to help them create a new branding identity and logo, to replace their original, dated brand logo.

We worked together to create something new that is based around a simple circle, creating custom lettering that is based on geometric shapes.

The new visuals reflect the luxurious and high-end nature of the company, and the navy and rose-gold colors lend it a rich and sophisticated feel.
