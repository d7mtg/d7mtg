Automate Cab Network

  SERVICES

Automate is a revolutionary cab service that completely automates the way you ride.

Starting by removing the need for a dispatcher, you can now book a cab directly by texting or calling the number, and the cab will pick you up and drop you off wherever you are.

Automate aims to make the ride as easy as possible, by updating its clients with the latest information about the cab and its driver.

D7mtg worked with Automate to create a logo and a brand identity for the company, and develop a full-scale marketing campaign to promote the company.

We designed their entire fleet of Tesla vehicles, each with a unique pattern printed on the back to make it easier to identify.
