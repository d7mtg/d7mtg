The One Event by Hamaspik

  EVENTS

When Hamaspik asked us to create the identity and design the essentials for their upcoming staff appreciation event, we knew this was going to be special.

Hamaspik employees are so dedicated to helping those in need, and Hamaspik wanted to go above and beyond to express how valuable they are to the organization.

The theme of the event was "The One," speaking to the fact every individual, every single member of the organization is vital to their mission.

We created a logo that worked in tandem with the luxurious decor, and we designed all deliverables for the event: from the emails sent to the employees weeks in advance, all the way to the gifts given to participants after the event.
