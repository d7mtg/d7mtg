Exhibit A, a brand that represents style, professionalism and modernity.

Using a lower case structural sans serif font, the brand name is displayed in a neat typeface. Balanced with weight and grace, the letters form a visually pleasing mark.

The square represents the “exhibits” or showcases, the product of the company, as well as the service of the company to create “out of the box” designs and styles.
