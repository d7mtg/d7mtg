Curesse Medi Spa

  SERVICES

In a world where challenges abound and mental health is precarious, self care is put on a pedestal and is recommended by all. A medispa is the perfect place where any man and woman can escape to in order to relax, destress and feel taken care of. Today more than ever the need for enhancing self care and in turn self esteem is high up on the priority list.

This brand showcases a harmonious blend of sophistication and warmth, both crucial for the success of the brand. Curesse medi spa is a spa that specializes in mental and physical wellness. It provides a peace of mind and a space for clients to focus on themselves and find the time and space to take care of themselves and their mental health.

This is the type of place you can come to after a long day when you need to destress and detox.

For the type, we wanted to achieve a look that was inviting, soft yet empowering. We used a lettering style that has a graceful feeling but still shows a strong presence.

For the icon, we started out with a circular shape to represent the idea of wholeness, completeness, and organization. We drew inspiration from the simple things, the physical space that surrounds us: the silhouette of a sofa - a place where we can feel comfortable and at ease; a leaf - a symbol of rejuvenation and growth.

The slogan "reclaim your glow" is a nod to the idea that life can be overwhelming sometimes, but that you can always find joy, excitement, and happiness.
