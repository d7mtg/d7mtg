DripDrop Kids

  PRODUCTS

Kids are messy. Messes are frustrating. Drip Drop disposable bibs are the perfect solution to your messy little problem. With a quick and easy disposable bib, you can stop the mess from ever being a problem again.

There are no spills, no dribbles, and no mess.

We created the logo with a youthful and playful font, and combined the letter D in the logo with a playful swirl that looks like a drop of water, symbolizing the simplicity and ease of the product.

The colors for the brand are soft pink and teal, and are inspired by the colors of a baby's nursery.
