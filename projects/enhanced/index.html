Created at: Pivot Group
Date completed: May 19, 2020

The apple is a powerful symbol of knowledge and education.

We softened the apple with rounded edges to make it child friendly and inviting while still maintaining a professional edge.

The typeface used is a clean sans serif that lends an educational vibe and portrays caring and trust.

The color combination chosen is blue and red.

Blue is a color that makes you feel safe and relaxed. Blue evokes feelings of calmness as well as security and trust.

Red, on the other hand, points toward strength, power, determination as well as passion, desire, and love.

Together, the two colors blend harmoniously to combine a relaxed feeling of trust and a fiery determination to succeed.
